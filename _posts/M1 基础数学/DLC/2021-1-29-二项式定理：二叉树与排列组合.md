$$ (x+y)^n $$

$$ (x+y)(x+y)\ldots(x+y) $$

$$ (x_1+y_1)(x_2+y_2)\ldots(x_n+y_n) $$

$$ x_1 \Big[(x_2+y_2)\ldots(x_n+y_n) \Big] + y_1 \Big[(x_2+y_2)\ldots(x_n+y_n) \Big] $$

$$ x_2 \Big[(x_3+y_3)\ldots(x_n+y_n) \Big] + y_2 \Big[(x_3+y_3)\ldots(x_n+y_n) \Big] $$

$$ (x+y)^n = \sum_{k=0}^n c(n,k) x^k y^{n-k} $$
